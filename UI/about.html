How secure is Politico

There are two guiding principles when it comes to software design philosophy: Make it so complex that nibody can really detect the flaw within, or make it so simple that there are no obvious flaw within.Politico was built in Python programming language and is now managed by team of great software engineers.However, Okoth Peter still maintains the legal rights and authority to direct it's progress as well as veto power to revert any changes that seems colloiqual.

With regard to security, Politico is designed to act as a final answer or fail-proof desing towards progress of interity. We use the latest encryption designs, cryptography and all measures of new securities that come in hand to keep of the entire voting system from the reach of even the best and most brilliant of all hackers. Although revealing all the particulars of our security system will be a great mess owing to sensitivity of the electoal system, be rest assured that everything is good in our hands.
